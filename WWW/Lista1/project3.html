Vulkan Project

GitHub

C++, Vulkan, GLSL, 3D geometry

3D model engine that gives the ability to build a world filled with 3D objects including aspects such as water rendering, terrain with height maps, light objects, normal mapped models, specular maps for objects, post-processing effects (gaussian blur, blume effect, contrast), and entity management system.
